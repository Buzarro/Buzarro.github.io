Recipe #1: Buttered Noodles

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2-4
Ingredients Quantity
Angel Hair Pasta Half a box
Butter Half a stick
Water A pot

Step 1: Bring water to a boil and put pasta in. Break in half if necessary.

Step 2: Boil until sticky and yummy. Test while scalding hot.

Step 3: Drain pot and put butter in, letting it melt at the bottom while also cooling your scalding hot pasta.

Step 4: Eat in a bowl with or without parmasen cheese
